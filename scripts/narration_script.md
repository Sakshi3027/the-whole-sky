# The Whole Sky — Narration Script v1

## SCENE 1 — OPEN
"I didn't leave because something was missing."
"I left because I wanted more."

## SCENE 2 — ARRIVAL
"More exposure. More challenge. More possibility."
"My parents told me — the whole sky is yours. Go and fly the way you want."
"So I did."

## SCENE 3 — BOSTON
"Boston was nothing like I imagined."
"The classrooms were different. The people were different."
"Even the way silence felt — was different."

## SCENE 4 — THE STRUGGLE
"There was a phase — not one day, but a phase — where working hard wasn't enough."
"I was surrounded by people who seemed so far ahead."
"That phase humbled me. And then it changed me."

## SCENE 5 — REBUILD
"I learned to ask for help. I learned to rebuild."
"I learned that uncertainty isn't the opposite of progress — it's part of it."

## SCENE 6 — HARVARD
"Then one afternoon at Harvard, I found myself in a room I couldn't have imagined two years ago."
"A real conversation. With someone who had built something that matters."

## SCENE 7 — CLOSE
"And for a moment, everything aligned."
"The risks. The leaving. The struggling. The rebuilding."
"It all led to that room."
"I'm not where I want to be yet."
"But I know now — I belong in spaces like this."
"The sky was always mine."
"I'm just learning how to fly."
